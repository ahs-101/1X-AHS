BRAND VOICE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Tone: Warm, human-centered, quietly confident. Never cold or tech-industrial.
The language is optimistic and emotionally accessible — words like "cozy,"
"gentle," "soft," "laugh," and "companion" dominate product copy. Technical
specs are presented but never foregrounded. The brand speaks to the home and
relationships, not efficiency or productivity.
 
Vocabulary examples from the site:
- "Transform Your Home"
- "Soft and Safe"
- "Laugh and Learn"
- "Grows With You"
- "Comfortable to be Around"
- "Reclaim Time"
- "Helpful Intelligence"
- "Automate Your Chores"
- "Experience Humanoid"
- "Experience NEO"
 
Voice archetype: Gentle innovator — intelligent but approachable, visionary
but grounded in everyday life.

COLORS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Overall Palette: Dark, cinematic, premium. The site uses a near-black or deep
charcoal background that creates a high-contrast canvas for full-bleed imagery.
 
Primary Background: Very dark gray / near-black (#0d0d0d or similar deep tone)
Text: White / off-white for headings; light gray for body copy
Accent: Warm natural tones — the robot is available in earthy/natural colorways
  - NEO Color Variants shown: Warm White / Cream, Warm Tan/Blush, and a
    darker Mocha/Brown tone (three colorways shown in hero)
UI Highlights: Minimal use of accent color; the photography and robot imagery
provide all warmth and color
Button style: High-contrast white text on dark or bordered buttons
Section labels (e.g. "UTILITY", "DESIGN", "COMPANION", "INTELLIGENCE"):
  rendered in small caps, spaced tracking, likely light gray or muted white

SHADERS / BACKGROUNDS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The site is heavy on full-bleed photography and video rather than CSS gradient
shaders. Key background treatments:
 
1. Full-bleed dark photography (hero, section backgrounds)
   — High-resolution images of NEO in nature (forest, outdoors, leaves)
   — Cinematic color grade: dark, moody, warm shadows
 
2. Full-viewport video thumbnails
   — Mux-hosted video embeds used as background/preview tiles
   — Play-button overlay with soft darkening vignette
 
3. Dark background sections
   — Pure or near-black flat backgrounds for spec tables and FAQ areas
   — White typography floats clean on dark surfaces
 
4. Split-section image+text layouts
   — Large image occupies 50–60% of width, text on dark side
   — Subtle fade or blur edges at transitions
 
5. Scroll-triggered image carousel / crossfade
   — Hero alternates between two images (animated webp or CSS transition)
 
No heavy particle systems, WebGL shaders, or generative backgrounds detected.
The visual depth comes entirely from photography and dark negative space.

FEATURES SECTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Organized under 4 major pillars (tab-based on /neo and /order):
 
── UTILITY ──
• Autonomy ("Get the door")
  NEO uses Redwood AI — 1X's Generalist AI model — for learning and repeating
  tasks. Arrives with basic autonomy and grows in capability over time.
 
• Scheduled Expert Mode ("Laundry")
  For complex tasks NEO doesn't know, a 1X Expert can remotely supervise its
  actions at scheduled times to help it learn and get the job done.
 
• Voice Interface
  Speak naturally to NEO to access all features without devices.
 
• Mobile Interface (NEO App)
  Manage chore schedule, communicate remotely, monitor NEO and more.
 
• Self-Charge
  NEO manages its own battery life. When it needs a charge — it plugs itself in.
 
• Remote Control
  Pilot NEO from anywhere via Mobile App & VR device.
 
• Emotive Ear Rings
  Communicate NEO's state and status (battery, attention, etc.).
 
• Boom Box
  Use NEO as a mobile Bluetooth speaker anywhere in the home.
 
── DESIGN ──
• Soft Body
  All hardware wrapped in custom 3D lattice polymer for safety and cushioning.
 
• Safe Movements
  1X Tendon Drive actuation creates precise, low-energy movements for home use.
 
• Pinch Proof
  Joints covered from outside access — surface entirely pinch proof.
 
• Body Language
  NEO understands and responds to human gestures and expressions.
 
• Comfortable to be Around
  Designed to complement your living space, not disrupt it.
 
• Machine Washable
  Soft suit and shoes made from machine washable nylon.
 
── ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ──
• Redwood AI
  Vision language model for learning and performing chores. [Learn more → /ai]
 
• Built-in LLM
  Interaction driven by large language model capable of understanding,
  reasoning, and conversing.
 
• Audio Intelligence
  Interprets audio cues and conversational context.
 
• Memory
  Retains information to personalize your experience.
 
• Visual Intelligence
  Uses visual input to enhance conversations.
 
• Fully Mobile
  Uses AI for navigating to where it's needed.
 
── HARDWARE (Specs) ──
Robot:
  Height: 5'6" | Weight: 66 lbs | Lift: 154 lbs | Carry: 55 lbs
  Arm Payload: 18 lbs
 
Degrees of Freedom:
  Hands: 22×2 | Arms: 7×2 | Neck: 3 | Spine: 2 | Legs: 6×2
 
Speed:
  Hands: 8.0 m/s | Max run: 6.2 m/s | Walking: 1.4 m/s
 
Battery:
  Capacity: 842 Wh | Run-time: 4h | Quick charge: 6 min per hour runtime
 
Compute:
  Chipset: 1X NEO Cortex (Nvidia Jetson Thor)
  AI Compute: Up to 2070 FP4 TFLOPS
  Microphone: 4 beamforming mics
  Speakers: 3-stage (pelvis and chest)
  Camera: Dual 8.85MP 90Hz Stereo Fisheye
  Communication: WiFi, Bluetooth, 5G
 
Safety:
  Body: Soft with custom lattice polymer | Joints: No pinchpoints
  HIC: <250 | Actuation: Low inertia tendon drives
 
Ingress Protection:
  Hands: IP68 (submersible) | Body: IP44 (splash proof)
 
Audible Noise: 22 dB
